About Our MUN Club

Developing leadership, diplomacy, and public speaking skills.

Meet Our President

Lucas Mejia

Lucas Mejia founded our Model United Nations Club in 2024 with the vision of creating a space where students could engage in diplomatic discussions, enhance their debate skills, and explore global issues.

Under his leadership, the club has grown into a platform for students to develop confidence, leadership, and public speaking skills through international conferences.

Lucas is now planning and working on behalf of the Servite Sector of the Model United Nations club, with a passion and goal to foresee its growth

Our Mission

Our goal is to empower students with the knowledge and confidence to engage in diplomatic discussions and address global challenges.
